CHEESEBURGERS

INGREDIENTS : ( 4 BURGERS )

4 Burger buns

4 Ground beef patties

8 Bacon slices

4 Cheddar slices

1 Onion

Fried onions

Burger sauce

Cajun spices

Salt / Pepper

PREPARATION

➤     In a hot pan without oil, cook 8 bacon slices.

➤     Once nicely browned, remove them.

➤     In the same pan, cook 4 ground beef patties, season with salt, pepper and Cajun spices, then flatten them and flip occasionally for even browning.

➤     Finely chop 1 small onion.

➤     Take your burger buns and cut them in half.

➤     Spread burger sauce on both sides of each bun.

➤     Assemble your burgers as follows :

             -     Bottom bun

             -     Chopped onion

             -     Beef patty

             -     1 cheddar slice

             -     2 bacon slices

             -     Fried onions

             -     Top bun

➤     Bake for 5 minutes at 180°C (350°F).

➤     Take them out of the oven and enjoy !
