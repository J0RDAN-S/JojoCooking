TARTIFLETTE

INGREDIENTS : ( 4 PEOPLE )

1 Reblochon ( 500 g )

1 kg Potatoes

200 g Bacon lardons

2 Onions

200 ml Cream

Salt / Pepper

PREPARATION

➤     Peel 1 kg of potatoes, then cut them into fairly large pieces.

➤     Cook them in boiling salted water for 15 minutes.

➤     Sauté 2 chopped onions in a knob of salted butter for a good 5 minutes over medium heat.

➤     Add the bacon lardons, season with pepper, and cook for 5 minutes.

➤     Add 200 ml of semi-thick cream and let infuse for 5 minutes.

➤     Place the potatoes in an oven dish.

➤     Pour the cream, onions and lardons over the top, then mix.

➤     Cut a Reblochon cheese in half lengthwise and place on top.

➤     In a preheated oven at 200 ° C (390 ° F), bake for about twenty minutes until the Reblochon crust is golden.

➤     Take the dish out of the oven and all that's left is to enjoy !
