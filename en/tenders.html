CHICKEN TENDERS

INGREDIENTS : ( 6 TENDERS )

6 Chicken tenderloins

200 ml Cream

Flour

Tabasco

Paprika

Chili powder

Garlic powder

Salt / Pepper

PREPARATION

➤     In a container, pour 200 ml of semi-thick cream.

➤     Season with salt, pepper, chili powder, paprika, and a few drops of Tabasco, then mix.

➤     Add the tenderloins, then let marinate in the refrigerator for a good 3 hours.

➤     In another container, add flour and the same spices used previously.

➤     Take a tenderloin, place it in the flour, dip it again in the cream, then again in the flour.

➤     Plunge it into oil ( 180 ° C / 350 ° F, medium heat ), and let cook for 10 minutes, turning occasionally.

➤     Once cooked, place on a rack or paper towels.

➤     Let cool for a few moments before enjoying !
