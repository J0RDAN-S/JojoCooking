JURASSIAN ESCALOPE

INGREDIENTS : ( 2 PEOPLE )

2 Veal escalopes

500 g Potatoes

2 Slices cured ham

50 g Grated cheese

200 ml Cream

50 g Salted butter

2 Garlic cloves

Mustard

Garlic powder

Onion powder

Chili flakes

Salt / Pepper

PREPARATION

➤     Cut the pre-washed potatoes into fairly large pieces.

➤     Cook them in boiling salted water for 15 minutes.

➤     In a bowl, melt 50 g of salted butter in the microwave, then add pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and chili flakes.

➤     Place the potatoes in an oven dish, then brush them with the spiced butter.

➤     In a preheated oven at 230 ° C (450 ° F), bake for 1 hour, turning them occasionally.

This step should be done when the potatoes have been in the oven for 45 minutes.

➤     Place the veal escalopes in a hot pan with salted butter, and let brown for 1 to 2 minutes per side.

➤     Place the escalopes in an oven dish.

➤     In the same pan, add 2 chopped garlic cloves, 200 ml of semi-thick cream, and 1 tablespoon of mustard.

➤     Mix and let reduce for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.

➤     On the escalopes, place the cured ham, then add a tablespoon of sauce, and finally sprinkle the grated cheese on top.

➤     Bake for 10 minutes at 230 ° C (450 ° F).

➤     Take the dish out of the oven.

➤     Arrange the potatoes and veal escalopes on 2 plates, then cover everything with the cream sauce.

➤     All that's left is to enjoy !
