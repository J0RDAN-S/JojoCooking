WARM GOAT CHEESE SALAD

INGREDIENTS : ( 1 PERSON )

100 g Salad greens

200 g Potatoes

100 g Bacon lardons

4 Breaded goat cheeses

1 Shallot

Vegetable oil

Red wine vinegar

Mustard

Parmesan cheese

Balsamic vinegar

Salt / Pepper

PREPARATION

➤     Peel 200 g of potatoes and cook in boiling salted water for 15 minutes.

➤     Cut the potatoes into cubes and sauté in salted butter in a hot pan.

➤     In the same pan, add the bacon lardons and let them brown.

➤     In the same pan, add the 4 breaded goat cheeses and brown each side.

➤     Finely chop one shallot.

➤     In a salad bowl, add 2 tablespoons of mustard, 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil, 3 tablespoons of red wine vinegar, pepper, and the chopped shallot, then mix.

➤     Add the salad greens, then toss.

➤     On a plate, arrange the salad, potatoes, lardons and breaded goat cheeses.

➤     Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and drizzle with balsamic vinegar, and all that's left is to enjoy !
