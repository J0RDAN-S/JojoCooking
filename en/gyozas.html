GYOZAS

INGREDIENTS : ( 22 GYOZAS )

Flour

Cornstarch

400 g Ground beef

1/2 Leek

1 Onion

4 Garlic cloves

Sweet soy sauce

Toasted sesame oil

Paprika

Salt / Pepper

PREPARATION

➤     In a large bowl, add 350 g of flour then pour 1 glass of warm water.

➤     Mix by hand until you get a more or less elastic dough ball ( Add water or flour if needed ).

➤     Knead the dough for 5 minutes, then cover with a dry cloth and let rest for 30 minutes.

➤     Finely chop 1 onion and ½ leek.

➤     In a container, add 400 g of ground beef, 4 chopped garlic cloves, 2 tablespoons of sweet soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of toasted sesame oil, salt, pepper, paprika.

➤     Mix, then add onion and leek, then mix again until ingredients are evenly distributed.

➤     Place in the refrigerator.

➤     Take your dough and form small balls about 2.5 cm in diameter.

➤     On your work surface, sprinkle cornstarch and prepare a small bowl of water nearby.

➤     Take a dough ball, dip it in water, then roll it in cornstarch.

➤     Using a rolling pin, flatten the dough thinly to form a 10 cm diameter disc.

➤     Dust the dough disc with cornstarch, place on a plate, then repeat these steps.

➤     Brush a large frying pan with sunflower oil ( or other vegetable oil ).

➤     Take a dough disc, moisten the edge of your disc with water using a brush, then place a heaping teaspoon of filling in the center of the disc.

➤     Fold the dough in half by pressing the top in the middle, then make 3 pleats on each side.

➤     Place it in the previously oiled pan, then repeat.

➤     Let brown for 5 minutes over medium heat.

➤     Pour water halfway up the gyozas, cover, then cook for 7 minutes.

➤     Remove the lid, turn to high heat and let the water evaporate for 2 to 3 minutes.

➤     Evenly distribute 2 tablespoons of toasted sesame oil over the gyozas.

➤     Leave on high heat for 6 to 7 minutes to caramelize and slightly burn the bottom of the gyozas.

➤     Grab your favorite condiment and get ready to enjoy !
