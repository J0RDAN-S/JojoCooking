CHICKEN NUGGETS

INGREDIENTS : ( 12 NUGGETS )

2 Chicken breasts

Flour

Cornstarch

Sparkling water

Paprika

Chili powder

Garlic powder

Salt / Pepper

PREPARATION

➤     Finely chop the chicken using a food processor or knife.

➤     Season with salt, pepper, paprika, chili powder and garlic powder, then mix.

➤     Divide the mixture into 12 portions, then shape your nuggets.

➤     Place your nuggets on parchment paper, then put in the freezer for about twenty minutes to make them easier to handle.

➤     Take out the nuggets and coat them with flour.

➤     In a container, add 150 g of flour, 2 tablespoons of cornstarch, and the same spices used previously.

➤     Gradually add sparkling water, then mix. You should obtain a waffle batter consistency.

➤     Coat your nuggets with this batter, then plunge them into oil ( 180 ° C / 350 ° F, medium heat ).

➤     Cook for 3 minutes on each side.

➤     Once cooked, place your nuggets on a rack or on paper towels.

➤     Let cool for a few moments, and it's time to eat !
