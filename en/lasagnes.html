LASAGNA

INGREDIENTS : ( 4 PEOPLE )

400 g Ground beef

Lasagna sheets

400 ml Tomato sauce

300 ml Béchamel sauce

2 Onions

3 Garlic cloves

100 g Parmesan cheese

100 g Grated cheese

Ketchup

Paprika

Cajun spices

Salt / Pepper

PREPARATION

➤     Sauté 2 chopped onions in salted butter in a pan over medium heat for 5 minutes.

➤     Add 3 chopped garlic cloves, mix and leave for an additional 2 minutes.

➤     In the same pan, add the ground beef. Season with salt, pepper, paprika and Cajun spices.

➤     Once the meat is cooked, add 400 ml of tomato sauce and 2 tablespoons of ketchup.

➤     Lower the heat and let simmer for a few moments to allow the flavors to infuse the sauce.

➤     In an oven dish, spread 2 tablespoons of béchamel sauce over the entire surface, and arrange the lasagna sheets.

➤     Then, repeat the following steps :

             -     Add ¼ of the bolognese sauce

             -     Spread a little béchamel sauce on top

             -     Sprinkle with parmesan cheese

             -     Arrange lasagna sheets

➤     Repeat these steps until you have no sauce left.

➤     On the last layer of pasta, pour the remaining béchamel sauce, add the remaining parmesan cheese, then add the grated cheese evenly.

➤     In a preheated oven at 180 ° C (350 ° F), bake for 1 hour. And all that's left is to enjoy !
